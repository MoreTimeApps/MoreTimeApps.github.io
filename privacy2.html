WHAT TYPE OF INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

The first type of information we collect is non-identifiable information pertaining to a user, which may be made available or gathered via the users’ installation or use of the App, and for which we are not aware of the identity of the user from which such non-personal information was collected (“Non-Personal Information”).

Non-Personal Information which is collected may include your aggregated usage information and technical information transmitted by your device, including certain software and hardware information, all as detailed below:

    For Google Analytics and Firebase:
  • Type of device, operating system, device model name, whether the device is rooted, screen size and mobile network code;
  • Your location (i.e., the country you are located in, but not your on-going data location);

    • Purpose of Google Analytics and Firebase SDKs: For analysing app performance, study app workflow, user actions and resolve crashes.

    For Hivex Solutions:
  • Collected data consists of geographical location, device model and type, OS version, connections to mobile network and to Wi-Fi, including, but not limited to, network diagnostic information, carrier frequency, connection quality, signal strength, direction, velocity, data rate, use statistics. We do collect large amount of statistics and measurements, however, we do not collect any identifiable personal information and we make efforts to ensure that our datasheets cannot be used to identify individual people or devices;

    • Purpose of Hivex Solutions SDK: This is a third party SDK team, Hivex Solutions publish open signal coverage maps on Websites and Applications. They help telecommunication companies to analyze their networks, understand trends in user and device behavior per location, plan and optimize signal coverage, create effective marketing campaigns. One of the most typical use case with our collected data is to identify locations of poor WiFi or cellular signals so that wireless service providers could fix their network issues and improve performance for their subscribers. To know more about Hivex Solutions and how they handle user's privacy, please visist: https://hivex-solutions.com/static/en/privacy.html

 

The second type of information is individually identifiable information, namely information that identifies an individual or may with reasonable effort allow the identification of an individual (“Personal Information”).
